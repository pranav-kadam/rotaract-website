About Us

About Us

Rotaract Club of WCE Sangli is one of the oldest clubs in WCE, established on February 23rd, 1975. Celebrating its 49th charter year this year, the club has been instrumental in enriching the community and students through numerous activities and events. For its members, the club aims to achieve personal, professional, and social objectives.

Our Pillars

The club's mission is founded upon four main pillars, known as our four avenues. Each avenue focuses on specific aspects of development and contributes to the growth of both our members and society.

  • Club Service Avenue
  • Community Service Avenue
  • International Service Avenue
  • Professional Development Service Avenue

These four avenues guide our activities and initiatives, ensuring a holistic approach to growth and empowerment.
